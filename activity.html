Rankings of Kings

Ranking of Kings (王様ランキング, Ōsama Rankingu) is a Japanese fantasy manga series written and illustrated by Sōsuke Tōka. It has been serialized online via Echoes' user-submitted Manga Hack website since May 2017 and has been collected in fourteen tankōbon volumes by Enterbrain.

The Devil is a part timer

The Devil Is a Part-Timer is a Japanese light novel series written by Satoshi Wagahara, with illustrations by Oniku (written as 029).

My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia (僕ぼくのヒーローアカデミア Boku no Hīrō Akademia?) is an anime series produced by Toho and Bones based on the manga by Kohei Horikoshi. It was announced in Issue 49, 2015 of Weekly Shonen Jump.
